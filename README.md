# Benchwarmers — Football Training App ⚽

Benchwarmers is a modern football training web application designed to help players improve their skills, learn tactics, and track their development. The concept is simple: every great player starts somewhere — even on the bench.

This app provides structured drills, playstyle-based training, tactical knowledge, and motivational tools to help players train smarter and become better footballers.

---

## Features

### Player Dashboard
- Track training progress
- View XP and achievements
- Monitor completed drills

### Play Style Based Training
Players can select a play style such as:

- Playmaker
- Speedster
- Target Man
- Defensive Wall
- Box-to-Box
- Dribble Artist

The app generates drills based on the selected play style.

### Training Programs
Structured football training sessions including:

- Warmups
- Skill drills
- Position-based exercises
- Cooldowns

### Pro Player Attribute Comparison
Compare your attributes with professional football players like:

- Lionel Messi
- Cristiano Ronaldo

Attributes include:

- Speed
- Passing
- Dribbling
- Shooting
- Stamina
- Strength

### Tactical Learning
Learn common football formations:

- 4-3-3
- 4-4-2
- 3-5-2
- 4-2-3-1

Includes formation explanations and player roles.

### Match Tactics
Understand modern tactical concepts such as:

- High pressing
- Counter attack
- Possession play
- Defensive blocks

### Recovery & Injury Prevention
Educational content including:

- Warmup routines
- Stretching exercises
- Recovery techniques
- Injury prevention tips

### Daily Humorous Motivation
A random football-themed motivational quote appears every day to keep players motivated.

Example:
> “Even legends started as benchwarmers.”
